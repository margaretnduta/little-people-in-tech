Our Mission & Vision

We are building a more inclusive tech industry through action, support, and innovation.

💚

Accessibility

Designing technology and spaces that are fully accessible and usable for people with all sensory and physical profiles.

💙

Inclusion & Support

Creating a supportive work environment and networking space built on trust, stability, and mutual intelligence.

🧡

Representation

Breaking down physical and professional barriers to create equal opportunity for people of short stature in tech.

💛

Innovation

Embracing neurodiversity and the unique perspectives and innovative thinking our community brings to the industry.
