Welcome to our safe, inclusive space. Before you jump in, here is how we build meaningful connections and support one another in the tech industry.

Community Guidelines

How to make the most out of your networking experience.

1

Lead with Empathy & Inclusion

We all come from different backgrounds. Assume positive intent, be respectful of different communication styles, and create a supportive environment for everyone, particularly when discussing mental health or workplace challenges.

2

Advocate for Accessibility

When sharing links, projects, or hosting virtual rooms, ensure your resources are accessible. Use alt-text for images, provide captions where possible, and prioritize universal design in your collaborations.

3

Embrace Unique Perspectives

Neurodiversity drives innovation. Be open to unconventional problem-solving methods and encourage out-of-the-box thinking. Share your unique workflow and celebrate the different ways our community approaches tech.

4

Take Action & Lift Others

Networking is a two-way street. Don't just ask for opportunities—share job postings, offer to review portfolios, and mentor others. Together, we break down professional barriers and increase our representation.
